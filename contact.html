Frequently Asked Questions

Is DiffLens really free?

Yes! DiffLens is completely free and open source. You can use it, modify it, and distribute it under the terms of our open source license.

How do you handle my code/data?

DiffLens processes everything client-side in your browser. Your code never leaves your machine or gets transmitted to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security.

Can I contribute to the project?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions of all kinds. Check out our contribute page for guidelines on how to get started.

What diff formats are supported?

Currently, DiffLens supports standard git diff format. We're working on adding support for other formats like unified diff, context diff, and more.

How can I report a bug?

The best way to report bugs is through our GitHub Issues. Please include as much detail as possible to help us reproduce and fix the issue.
